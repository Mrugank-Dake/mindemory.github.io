About Me

Hi, I am Mrugank Dake and I am doing my PhD at New York University in Clayspace Lab working on understanding the role of visual cortex in working memory.

I have completed my Bachelors and Masters Dual Degree from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Tirupati in 2021. I have worked on a range of topics in the domains of Synthetic biology, Cancer, Neurobiology, Computational Neuroscience, Ecoacoustics and Machine Learning.

When not having my nth cup of coffee or working with data, I love playing Chess, travelling and reading books.
